Herbal Medicine Is Very Powerful and Effective

Herbal medicine is very powerful and effective for treating chronic interstitial cystitis (Hunner’s ulcers). Interstitial cystitis (IC) is a chronic bladder condition that causes bladder pressure, bladder pain, and sometimes pelvic pain. The pain ranges from mild discomfort to severe. IC is also known as painful bladder syndrome.

Hunner’s ulcers (also called Hunner’s lesions) are patches of inflammation and broken skin found on the bladder wall in some people with interstitial cystitis. These lesions can cause significant pain and urinary symptoms that severely impact quality of life.
