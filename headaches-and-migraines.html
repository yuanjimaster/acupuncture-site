Acupuncture for Headaches and Migraines

The pain that headache and migraine sufferers endure can impact every aspect of their lives. Intense pain, sensitivity to light and sound, and nausea are all an inconvenient possibility when suffering a headache or a migraine.

Acupuncture offers powerful relief without the side effects that prescription and over-the-counter drugs can cause. Acupuncture and Chinese herbal medicine have been able to relieve these pains, and their underlying causes, for thousands of years.

Finding the Root Cause

Practitioners must examine root causes for lasting relief. Chronic headaches and migraines are sometimes the result of something as simple and benign as a tense neck. Sometimes they're the result of something more insidious, such as a hormone imbalance.

Case Study

A patient suffering from chronic headaches and tinnitus had undergone MRI and CT-scans without diagnosis before seeking treatment at Dr. Huo's clinic.

Dr. Huo identified a blockage of energy through his body, preventing energy from moving from the head to the central part of his body. Treatment involved needle placement in specific areas, resulting in symptom improvement over three sessions.

The patient experienced significant improvement after the first session, with complete resolution of tinnitus by the third treatment.
